Hello! I'm Tyler Moeller.


I am passionate about languages and technology. I studied Linguistics at the University of Washington and lived in Spain for two years as I studied Spanish Language and Literature and taught English as a second language. I eventually decided to come back home to Seattle and start a career in technology. I was hired as a Test Engineer at Microsoft and became a people manager, leading teams of 3-15+ engineers delivering Windows, Microsoft Speech, Exchange, Dynamics CRM, and Skype for Business.
After nearly 11 years with Microsoft, I decided to take a break from my career - a big risk with the plan of one step back and two steps forward. I spent time with family, remodeled my home, learned to play the ukulele, explored new hobbies, and traveled throughout Europe, Latin America, and the United States. I also began exploring opportunities as a self-employed Technical Consultant for small businesses and individuals. Most of my time involves educating my clients about technology, administering their servers and websites, and enhancing their social media presence. I've lived in Seattle, Washington for over 30 years and I'll never be able to get enough of our trees, mountains, delicious coffee, craft beer, and technology. Follow my adventures on Twitter. If you are interested in learning more about me professionally, please feel free to connect on LinkedIn.
